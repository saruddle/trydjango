About Great American Natural Pet Food LLC

Great American Natural Pet Food LLC is an Indiana based limited liability company. The founders discovered the benefits of a raw diet through personal experience. After feeding their own pets a raw diet for over five years they knew that they had more energy, better digestion, shinier coats and softer skin. In short, their pets were healthier and much happier than when they were fed a diet of dry kibble. Now, the goal of Great American Natural Pet Food LLC is to provide a natural and convenient way for other pet owners to feed a raw diet to their four-footed family members at a reasonable price.
