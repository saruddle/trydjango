About Our Products

Great American Natural Pet Food consists of organic, hormone free, antibiotic free, grass fed meat as well as bone matter, organs, fruits and vegetables. We do not use any additives or fillers. We have plain ground (all meat/bone) and mixed grind (inc. fruits & veggies). Beef, Chicken, Pork, Turkey, Duck, Tripe and Rabbit. You'll find the same great ingredients in our treats as well.

One of the drawbacks to a raw pet diet is handling the food. In order to eliminate this headache we offer convenient, freezer safe, resealable 2-lb deli style tubs.

What We Offer

Our pet food is made with human grade quality food and inspected to human grade standards. The entire process takes place in a refrigerated environment so bacteria never have a chance to grow. Our pet food is "frozen fresh" - meaning it is frozen immediately after grinding and packaging. The freshness is locked in.
